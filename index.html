The Beginning

I met Tanvi on April 12, 2024. The moment I stepped inside, the warm atmosphere wrapped around me like a comforting blanket. That’s when I first saw Tanvi. She was sitting at a small table by the window with her friend, wearing an easy smile that instantly caught my attention. We locked eyes for just a moment, and I felt a spark of curiosity light up inside me. With the nerve that only a hopeful heart can muster, I introduced myself. To my surprise, conversation flowed effortlessly. We talked about everything—work, dreams, favorite songs, random hopes for the future—as though we’d been friends for ages rather than strangers who’d just met. Something about Tanvi’s presence felt… special. Even the clinking of glasses and laughter from nearby tables seemed to fade away. By the time we said our goodbyes, I realized Therapy had given me more than just a pleasant evening—it had delivered a life-changing encounter. A few hours later, I found myself at a Diljit concert, excitement buzzing through the crowd. The lights flared, the bass thumped, and the collective energy of the audience was electric. In the middle of that thrilling chaos, I spotted a familiar figure dancing with carefree abandon: Tanvi in (GA). I realized that Tanvi wasn’t just someone I’d met randomly—she was someone I was truly meant to find.
